5. Calculation Engine

Monthly Proration (proration.js)

For each employee × each month:
factor = hireTermOverlap(hireDate, termDate, month)
base = (salary / 12) × factor
bonus = base × bonusMatrix[seniority][function]
benefits = base × benefitsMatrix × countryMult
capex = (base + bonus + benefits) × capPct
opex = total - capex

P&L Walk Aggregation (landing.js)

HC Active headcount per period
C&B = Σ(base + bonus + benefits) all employees
OAO = Σ vendorRows + teRows monthly
CTR = Σ contractorRows monthly
D&A = depreciation schedule total
EBITDA = C&B + OAO + CTR + T&E
Tot Inv = EBITDA (total investment)
